# Causal publication policy

| Situation | Claim state |
|---|---|
| Direct event-specific outcome evidence | `observed_effect` |
| Curated mechanism, condition met, no direct outcome evidence | `established_mechanism` |
| Curated mechanism, condition unmet | `conditional_pathway` |
| Adequately sourced AI candidate | `emerging_signal` |
| Unsupported or unresolved contradictory evidence | `not_shown` |

Threat-only events do not satisfy a disruption condition. Their associated
curated mechanisms remain visible as conditional pathways that name the unmet
condition.
